Who am I?

I'm Matthew Michelson and I'm studying a Bachelor of Design Innovation, Majoring in Interaction Design at Victoria University of Wellington. I chose to take the Webdesign course as I have an interest in learning coding for websites & apps and hoping to improve my skills into other kinds of programming. I think it's a really cool way to express your creativity in a way that people can experience for themselves.
